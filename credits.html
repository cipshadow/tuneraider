TUNERAIDER • Bad Bunny • 8-Bit Music

Built on

The Game Boy 8-bit sound engine and MIDI playback come from b1rdmania/motif (the WARIO SYNTH engine) by @b1rdmania.

TUNERAIDER is a quiz game built entirely on top of their synthesis work — none of this plays a note without it.
